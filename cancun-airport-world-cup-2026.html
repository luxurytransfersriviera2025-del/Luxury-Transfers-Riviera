❓ FREQUENT QUESTIONS

World Cup 2026
Transfer FAQ

How far is Cancun Airport from the Hotel Zone? +

The Hotel Zone (Zona Hotelera) is approximately 20–25 minutes from the airport. Playa del Carmen is about 45–55 min, Tulum about 1h 40 min. All transfers are direct — no stops, no shared vans.

Do you offer group rates for World Cup fans? +

Yes! Our Group Van (up to 13 pax) and Family Van (up to 10 pax) are perfect for fan groups. Use the quote tool above for instant pricing — no surcharges for group vehicles. Need more than 13 pax? Contact us via WhatsApp and we'll arrange multiple vehicles.

Can you transfer us to the game cities (CDMX, GDL, MTY)? +

We specialize in Cancun Airport and Riviera Maya transfers. For the game cities, the easiest option is to fly from CUN — direct flights to Mexico City, Guadalajara and Monterrey take about 2 hours. We'll drop you at the airport and pick you up when you return.

What happens if my flight is delayed? +

We monitor all incoming flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, we automatically adjust your pickup time — no extra charge. Just keep your WhatsApp available and we'll keep you updated every step of the way.

Should I book in advance for the World Cup period? +

Absolutely — and as early as possible. The June–July 2026 period will be extremely busy in Cancun with World Cup traffic. Group vehicles especially fill up fast. We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle and time slot. For last-minute bookings, we accept requests with a minimum of 3 hours' notice.

Does the transfer include real-time flight tracking? +

Yes. We monitor your flight in real time. If it arrives early or is delayed, your driver adjusts automatically — no extra waiting charges. You'll receive your driver's WhatsApp number in advance for peace of mind.

Can I cancel or modify my booking? +

Yes. We accept date and time changes at no cost with at least 24 hours' notice. For cancellations, our standard policy applies based on how far in advance you cancel. Message us on WhatsApp and we'll handle it right away.

Do you operate late-night or early-morning transfers? +

Yes. We operate 24/7 — including overnight pickups for early flights or late arrivals. No additional charges for night or early-morning hours.

Do you accept USD or credit card payments? +

Yes. We accept Mexican pesos and US dollars. Available payment methods are: cash, payment link, or online payment when booking.

Do you offer hotel-to-hotel transfers within the Riviera Maya? +

Yes. In addition to airport transfers, we offer rides between hotels and destinations across the Riviera Maya: Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Tulum, Akumal, Puerto Morelos, and more.

Do you work with tour groups or travel agencies? +

Yes. We have special rates for travel agencies, organized groups, and World Cup fan packages. Message us on WhatsApp for a custom quote for your group.

How do I find my driver when I arrive? +

Your driver will be waiting at the arrivals area of Cancun Airport holding a sign with the company logo. Before your flight, you'll receive their WhatsApp number to coordinate in real time.
