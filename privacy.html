01 Booking Process and Payments
Booking Confirmation

Bookings are considered confirmed solely and exclusively upon a successful 100% online payment via our authorized gateways (Stripe or PayPal). Cash payments and partial deposits are not accepted to confirm or block a vehicle in our schedule.

Payment Security

Luxury Transfers Riviera (legally operated by Silver's Shuttle Services S.A. de C.V.) does not store credit or debit card data. All financial transactions are securely encrypted and processed directly on Stripe and PayPal servers, both holding PCI-DSS security certifications.

02 Cancellation and Refund Policies
Cancellations more than 24 hours in advance

A 70% refund of the total paid amount applies. The refund will be issued to the original payment method and will be reflected in your bank statement within 5 to 10 business days, depending strictly on your banking institution.

Cancellations less than 24 hours or No-Show

No refund or future credit will apply under any circumstances.

Force Majeure

The company is not liable, nor will it issue refunds, for delays or missed flights resulting from situations beyond our control, such as: heavy traffic, third-party accidents on public roads, road blockades, extreme weather, hurricanes, or other natural phenomena common to the region.

03 Waiting Times and Tolerance
Airport Arrivals

The maximum complimentary waiting time for passengers is 60 minutes, starting from the actual landing time of the flight. After 61 minutes with no contact from the passenger, the vehicle may leave the terminal, and the service will automatically be marked as a No-Show (no refund).

Hotel or Airbnb Pickups

The maximum waiting tolerance is 20 minutes from the scheduled pickup time on your confirmation. After this time, the vehicle may depart.

05 Insurance and Civil Liability
Traveler's Insurance

All passengers aboard our vehicles are covered by a Traveler's Civil Liability Insurance Policy, in strict compliance with the laws of the Mexican Secretariat of Infrastructure, Communications, and Transportation (SICT).

Medical Disclaimer

The company is fully exempt from lawsuits, additional compensation, or liability for health complications or deaths resulting from natural causes or pre-existing medical conditions of the passengers.

06 Cleaning Fees and Damages
Sanitation Fee

The client agrees to an automatic and unappealable charge of $300 MXN ($17 USD) for deep cleaning in the event of vomiting, excessive food or drink spills, or severe stains on the vehicle's upholstery.

Physical Damage

Any physical damage, whether intentional or accidental, caused to the vehicle by passengers will be billed to the client for the full cost of repair at an authorized shop.

07 Invoicing (Mexican CFDI)
Only for National Clients (Mexico)

If you require a Mexican tax invoice, you must request it by emailing reservas@luxurytransfersriviera.com.mx within the same calendar month your service took place. You must attach your updated Constancia de Situación Fiscal (PDF), specify the CFDI Use, payment method, and include your booking number. Invoices for previous months will not be issued.
